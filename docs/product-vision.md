# Product Vision

Experience Study AI Copilot is an actuarial workflow copilot designed to accelerate first-draft experience study analysis.

The product helps actuaries move from source data to validated Actual-to-Expected (A/E) outputs, interpretation-ready exhibits, and report-ready draft language while preserving a clear human review boundary.

## What It Helps With

- Deterministic A/E workflow execution.
- Interpretation of validated cohort-level outputs.
- Report section drafting.
- Methodology documentation.
- Workflow handoff through editable and exportable artifacts.

## What It Does Not Replace

- Actuarial peer review.
- Final actuarial sign-off.
- Assumption setting.
- Pricing decisions.
- Underwriting strategy recommendations.

The actuary remains the ultimate reviewer, owner, and signatory.

## Product Direction

The near-term goal is to keep the existing deterministic workflow stable while adding clearer governance, stronger golden workflow tests, auditable methodology tracking, and a safe AI interpretation layer.

The AI layer should interpret validated outputs and draft supporting language. It should not perform actuarial calculations, create unsupported conclusions, or replace professional judgment.
